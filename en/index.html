About

PhD student in Computer Science, and Researcher at UPV.

Graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Systems from the Universidad Autónoma de Chiapas (UNACH). Master's Degree in Systems Engineering from Universidad del Valle de México (UVM). Master's Degree in Computer and Network Engineering from the Universitat Politècnica de València (UPV).

I have participated in R&D projects related to Community Netwoks, ICT4D, Smart Cities and Internet of Things. Designing and developing communication network solutions. My research activity is related to the use of mobile wireless networks for the design of dynamic systems.

I consider myself curious about technology, perseverant and collaborative.

Currently, I am studying for my PhD in computer science at the UPV, in the Computer Networks Group (GRC).
